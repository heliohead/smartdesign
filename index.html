About

Today the Better is one of the most important companies on the international furniture market. Brilliantly successful beginnings have led the Better to make continuous research for quality and style. New products reflect changes in lifestyles and contemporary trends.

Who We Are

A group of highly skilled, experienced Italian craftsmen founded a co-op located in New York to mass produce wooden furniture. In just a few years The Better became an important industry leader on the woodworking market.

Our Philosophy

Innovation has always been a part of the production culture at the Better. The group's objective is the end-customer satisfaction.
The Better concentrates style and technological research on specific designs targeted at guaranteeing maximum quality.
